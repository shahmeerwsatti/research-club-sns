The SuperNova Research Club

The SuperNova Research Club was founded in 2024 due to the efforts of the student council in 2024. The club is the first of its kind in Islamabad, and has provided students access to a variety of opportunities and new extracurriculars, enabling them to achieve their full potential and providing students access to new opportunities not typically available to them!
